Construction Services

Sciemec provides a full spectrum of building and civil works services – from greenfield projects to extensions and renovations.

Building Construction

We execute complete building projects from foundations to roof, including structural works, walls, roofing, finishes and external works.

  • New schools, offices, clinics and community buildings
  • Extensions and alterations to existing facilities
  • Structural repairs and strengthening works
  • Internal finishes: tiling, ceilings, painting, partitions

Science Laboratories & Education Facilities

We understand the special requirements of science and technical spaces. Working closely with education stakeholders, we deliver laboratories and workshops that are safe, functional and aligned to teaching needs.

  • Science laboratories for primary and secondary schools
  • Preparation rooms, chemical stores and teacher resource rooms
  • Integration of services: gas, water, electrical points, safety equipment
  • Adaptation of existing classrooms into practical science spaces

Civil Works & Infrastructure

Robust civil works are the backbone of durable buildings. Sciemec executes the civil components that ensure long-term performance.

  • Excavation, foundations and reinforced concrete works
  • Slabs, plinths, ramps and platforms
  • Stormwater drainage and basic roadworks within sites
  • Retaining structures and basic earthworks

Project Management & Coordination

Every project needs clear planning and coordination. We provide project management support to keep scope, cost and timelines under control.

  • Work programming and scheduling
  • Site supervision and quality control
  • Cost and materials monitoring
  • Coordination with consultants, suppliers and inspectors

Need a Construction Partner?

Share your drawings, bill of quantities or concept with us, and we will help you shape it into a buildable, costed project.
