Turnkey Fixed Science Laboratories

Sciemec delivers complete science lab buildings – from foundations and structure to benches, services and equipment – aligned to the ZIMSEC and Cambridge syllabi.

Lab types we build

  • Dedicated chemistry, physics and biology labs.
  • Combined science / multi-purpose labs.
  • Preparation rooms and chemical stores.
  • Demonstration rooms and lecture-style labs.

Construction scope

  • Civil works, foundations and superstructure.
  • Lab-grade floors, wall finishes and ceilings.
  • Benches, cupboards, fume cupboards & sinks.
  • Gas, water, drainage and electrical installations.
  • Safety equipment – eye wash, fire extinguishers, signage.

Working with Sciemec

  • We review your site plan, enrolment and curriculum to propose suitable lab sizes and numbers.
  • We prepare concept drawings and a detailed BOQ or quotation.
  • Once approved, our team manages construction, installation and commissioning.
  • Teacher training on safe use of new facilities and equipment.
