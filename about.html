About Sciemec

Smart Science Labs & Construction

Who We Are

Sciemec is a Zimbabwean engineering and educational technology company that designs, manufactures, and installs modern science laboratories and mobile lab units.

Our Mission

To revolutionize science education in Zimbabwe and Africa by providing world-class STEM learning environments for primary, secondary and tertiary institutions.

What We Do

  • Turnkey construction of fully equipped science laboratories
  • Design & installation of gas, water & electrical laboratory systems
  • Manufacturing mobile science labs for rural & urban schools
  • Supplying science equipment, consumables & furniture
  • Teacher training and continuous technical support
