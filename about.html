About Sciemec Construction

A Zimbabwean construction company focused on high-quality buildings, science facilities and infrastructure that support education, industry and communities.

Who We Are

Sciemec Construction is a professional construction company based in Harare, providing building and civil engineering services to public, private and development sector clients. We work across education, health, commercial, industrial and residential projects.

Our teams bring together experienced engineers, site supervisors, artisans and project managers who understand both the technical demands of construction and the realities of delivering projects in Zimbabwean conditions.

Our Vision & Values

  • Quality: Deliver durable, safe and functional facilities that serve users for many years.
  • Integrity: Transparent communication, honest costings and accountable project management.
  • Innovation: Practical solutions that respond to local constraints while meeting modern standards.
  • Partnership: Working closely with clients, consultants and suppliers to achieve shared goals.

Our Experience Focus Areas

Education Facilities

Classrooms, administration blocks, science labs and technical workshops that support modern teaching and learning.

Health & Community

Clinics, small hospitals, community centres and refurbishment projects improving access to essential services.

Commercial & Industrial

Warehouses, workshops, office spaces and support buildings for businesses and industry.
