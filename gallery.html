What We Do

From bare classrooms to fully functional science centres, Sciemec provides complete solutions for safe, modern and inspiring learning spaces.

Mobile Science Laboratories

Compact, fully equipped labs mounted on trailers or vehicles, ideal for reaching rural and peri-urban schools that lack permanent infrastructure.

Fixed Lab Design & Construction

Turnkey civil works, benches, gas, water, electrical, storage and safety systems for chemistry, physics and biology laboratories.

Equipment & Consumables

Carefully curated packages of apparatus, models, glassware and chemicals aligned with local and international syllabi.

Service & Upgrades

Refurbishment of old laboratories, safety upgrades, equipment calibration and ongoing technical support.

Designed for Real Classrooms

Every Sciemec solution is built with the realities of African schools in mind: limited budgets, large class sizes, unreliable utilities and the need for durable, easy-to-maintain infrastructure.

  • Robust materials and fittings for high-usage environments
  • Flexible layouts for classes, exams and practical work
  • Safety-first designs: fume handling, emergency equipment and signage
  • Options for solar and backup power on mobile labs
