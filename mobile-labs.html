Mobile & Modular Science Laboratories

Our mobile labs are fully equipped teaching laboratories built into trailers or modular units. They bring practical science to schools that have limited infrastructure, especially in remote or high-density areas.

What’s inside a Sciemec mobile lab

  • Workbenches and stools for a full class (adapt to your profile).
  • Gas or alternative heat sources for experiments.
  • Water storage, sinks and basic waste management.
  • Secure storage for chemicals, glassware and apparatus.
  • Built-in power distribution, lighting and safety signage.

Perfect for

  • District-wide STEM outreach programmes.
  • Rural schools preparing for ZIMSEC & Cambridge exams.
  • Temporary labs while new school buildings are under construction.
  • Corporate or NGO-sponsored science programmes.

Delivery process

  • Needs assessment with the Ministry / DSI / school leadership.
  • Selection of lab layout (single, double or multi-purpose lab).
  • Manufacture of the mobile unit in our workshop.
  • On-site delivery, commissioning and teacher orientation.
